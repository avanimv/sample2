Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep is a union territory of India. It is an archipelago of 36 islands in the Arabian sea, located 200 to 440 km off the Malabar Coast. The name Lakshadweep means "one lakh islands" in Tamil and Sanskrit, though the Laccadive Islands are just one part of the archipelago of no more than a hundred islands.Jeseri is the primary as well as the widely spoken native language in the territory.
